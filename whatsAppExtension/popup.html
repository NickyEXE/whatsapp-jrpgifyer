Clicking the below button will activate the JRPG transformer.

You'll be prompted to choose your character (this avoids breaking the app), then you'll be sent back to WhatsApp.

After that, clicking on a message will start the story at that minute! So start where you want to begin reading.

You can also set it to follow messages in real time.

Going up will break you out of real time, while sending a message will put you into real time.
